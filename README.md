Amazon India Sales Performance Analysis (March to June 2022)

Overview
End-to-end sales performance analysis of an Amazon India marketplace dataset sourced from Kaggle. The analysis investigates confirmed revenue drivers, cancellation behaviour, and operational risk across geographic, category, size, fulfilment, and promotional dimensions.

Key Findings:
93% of total order value comes from confirmed orders
Five states contribute 56.41% of confirmed revenue
Set and Kurta alone account for over 75% of category revenue
Fulfilment channel shows no systemic cancellation risk
A single Free Shipping promotion drives 45.26% of confirmed orders

Analytical Framework:
A custom Cancellation Pressure Score (CPS) was developed to compare the proportional distribution of confirmed and cancelled orders across each dimension. A negative CPS indicates a state, category, or size is contributing disproportionately to cancellations relative to its revenue share.
